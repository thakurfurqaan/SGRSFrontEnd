About Us

We at Student Grievance Support System connect the Students to the Management System, which enables the respective bodies to address the difficulties/issues faced by the student in an efficient way and find a best possible solution to it ensuring student Satisfaction. The basic motive of this portal is to ensure transparency on various levels and provide a mechanism for redressal of the grievances.

The Student Grievance Support System is a great initiative taken by the government of Andhra Pradesh in order to facilitate students/ Complainants to lodge a grievance if they are facing any difficulty or issues with respect to Admission, Infrastructure, Examination, Finance etc.

The portal acts as a great platform for students to convey their problems to the concerned authorities and also being informed about the status of the same. It also helps the Redressal Bodies to function smoothly and efficiently, ensuring a hassle-free grievance resolution procedure. The system also facilitates the Redressal Bodies of respective levels (University, Institute, Department) to search and browse grievances lodged against their level (University, Institute, Department) and post action taken against the complaints on the portal as well as contact the complainant directly through an interactive one to one chat. The portal is a user-friendly, robust and an interactive web-application that ensures a great user experience and also acts a bridge connecting the Students and the Management System. Read more
