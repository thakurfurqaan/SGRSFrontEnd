FAQs

The portal has been created to provide convenience to student by allowing them to lodge a grievance through their mobile phones or computers. This removes the need for them to appear at any location and file a physical grievance. In addition, it provides them the facility of tracking the live status of their complaint.


The objective of the portal is to ensure speedy, convenient and effective redressal of student issues by the administration.


You can post grievances related to your department, college or university.

About Us

In todays time it is necessary that the students should be able to communicate and tell their grievances and problems to the concerned authorities so that a necessary action is taken for the betterment. Most of the time this is not possible due to several reasons.Sometimes due to fear or the lack of cofidence to speak up to the authority the problems remain unheard. Also to improve the quality of the institution it becomes necessary for the bodies to know what are the needs of the students.For this there is a requirement of some middle body that can interfere and help the students and the authoratative bodies to help them communicate with eachother.Hence we at Student Grievance Support Portal aim to provide the students a platform when they can put up the Grievances and the Redressaal body can efficiently respond to the respective Grievances hence making the resolving process very easy.
