News

We are a group of enthusiasts comprising of students and working professionals from IIT Bombay and beyond coming from the fields ranging from engineering, management, biosciences to Humanities and Social Sciences who are passionate towards the issues of social justice. We have come together to make the navigation of the relief process easy in the times of Covid19 crisis. There is a barrage of information circulating around regarding the relief programmes; on the one hand, it is a kind and responsible gesture, however, on the other, at times, the people who actually need relief are not able to navigate the enormous amount of information available and reach the suitable relief providers in their vicinity. So, we decided to create a common platform to bring together the people who need relief, people (Govt. initiatives/NGOs/groups/individuals) who are already providing the relief and the ones who want to volunteer in such relief programmes.

Purpose: The portal has been created to provide convenience to student by allowing them to lodge a grievance through their mobile phones or computers. This removes the need for them to appear at any location and file a physical grievance. In addition, it provides them the facility of tracking the live status of their complaint. Objective: The objective of the portal is to ensure speedy, convenient and effective redressal of student issues by the administration.
